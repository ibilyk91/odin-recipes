Sushi

Description

Japanese delicacy made with vinegared rice and topped with seafood. The Samurai's favorite food!

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori
  • Raw fish
  • Wasabi

Steps

  1. Cook and season the rice: Rinse rice, cook it, then mix with rice vinegar, sugar and salt to season it.
  2. Prepare toppings: Slice fresh fish (like tuna or salmon) and prepare vegetables(like cucumber or avocado) into thin strips.
  3. Assemble the sushi: Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat, spread a thin layer of rice on top, add fillings, then roll tightly.
  4. Slice and serve: Cut the roll into bite-sized pieces and serve with soy sauce and pickled ginger.
